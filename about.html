Our Story

We Ride Cycling Club was created by expats for expats. We understood the challenges involved in moving abroad, trying to discover the best cycling routes and the struggle of finding a new cycling group. It can be daunting to explore unknown roads and with that in mind we started to develop a network of local cycling experts throughout Europe and an exclusive cycling route database.

Our People

At the core of We Ride Cycling Club are our “Local Leaders”. Each Local Leader has over 10 years of cycling experience in their country and know every hill, corner, and bump in the road like the back of their hand. They are passionate about helping expats discover their new country and lead weekly rides for our community members.

Mission and Values

Our mission is to connect cycling enthusiasts across Europe and help them discover their new home in the best way possible, on two wheels. We operate on the basis of three core values:

Community

We unite and bring people together.

Inclusion

We believe that cycling is a sport for everyone.

Adventure

We encourage you to explore and discover.
